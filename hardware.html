    Supported Target Hardware

    As of version 0.23, VIC™ supports the following Microchip PIC® microcontrollers:

    PIC12 family: In Development

    • P12F683

    PIC16 family:

    • P16F627A
    • P16F628A
    • P16F631
    • P16F648A
    • P16F677
    • P16F685
    • P16F687
    • P16F689
    • P16F690

    PIC18 family: In Development

    • P18F242
    • P18F252
    • P18F442
    • P18F452
    • P18F13K50
    • P18F14K50
    • P18LF13K50
    • P18LF14K50
